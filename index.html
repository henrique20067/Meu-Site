Unraveling Accounting:

The Accounting Technician will be qualified to:

  • Balance Sheet;
  • Income Statement;
  • Accounting Bookkeeping;
  • Cash Flow Statement;
  • Perform administrative and accounting processes;
  • Classify accounting, tax, and non-tax documents;
  • Calculate federal, state, and municipal taxes;
  • Provide inspection assistance and present documents, books, and accounting reports;
  • Order accounting facts by debit and credit;
  • Determine legal assets, rights, and obligations.

To work as an Accounting Technician, the following are essential:

  • Knowledge related to business financial and accounting processes, in order to act in accordance with legislation and guidelines from regulatory bodies, as well as occupational health and safety standards, always under the supervision of an accountant.
  • Action based on responsible decisions based on constructive ethical concepts and positive relationships, teamwork, and effective conflict resolution.

Field of activity:

  • Service Provision Companies.
  • Consulting and/or Auditing firms under the supervision of an accountant.
  • Action based on responsible decisions based on constructive ethical concepts and positive relationships.
